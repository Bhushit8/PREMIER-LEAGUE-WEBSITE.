Copa America
2021

CONMEBOL Copa América 2021 was the 47th edition of the prestigious South American international football tournament, held in Brazil from June 13 to July 10, 2021. Argentina won the tournament, defeating Brazil 1-0 in the final, marking their first Copa América title since 1993.

Neymar Jr

Neymar is a Brazilian forward celebrated for his flair, creativity, and dynamic playing style. He is known for his dribbling skills and ability to score spectacular goals. Neymar has played for top clubs like Barcelona and Paris Saint-Germain, and he is a key player for the Brazilian national team.

Lionel Messi

Known as one of the greatest footballers in history, Lionel Messi has an exceptional ability to dribble past defenders and create opportunities for his teammates. He has a remarkable record of goals and assists across his illustrious career, predominantly spent with Barcelona before moving to Paris Saint-Germain. Messi is also an influential leader on the field and helped Argentina win the Copa América in 2021. lectus

Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese forward renowned for his athleticism, goal-scoring ability, and versatility on the field. His illustrious career includes successful stints at clubs such as Manchester United, Real Madrid, and Juventus. Ronaldo's leadership and competitive drive have made him one of the most successful footballers in history.

Paulo Dybala

Di Maria

Ángel Di María is an Argentine winger known for his pace, creativity, and ability to deliver precise crosses. He has played for top clubs such as Real Madrid, Manchester United, and Paris Saint-Germain.

Kylian Mbappé

Kylian Mbappé is a French forward known for his lightning speed, goal-scoring prowess, and exceptional dribbling. At a young age, he has already achieved significant success, including winning the FIFA World Cup with France in 2018.
